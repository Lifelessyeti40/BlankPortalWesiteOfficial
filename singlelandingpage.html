Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered. Learn more about our web development services and how we can help bring your ideas to life.

What is a landing page? ▾

A landing page is a standalone web page designed to capture leads or convert visitors through a specific call-to-action.

Why should I have a custom landing page? ▾

Custom landing pages are tailored to your brand and goals, maximizing conversions and creating a seamless user experience.

What’s the difference between a landing page and a multi-page website? ▾

A landing page focuses on a single goal, like capturing leads or promoting a product. A multi-page website provides comprehensive information about your business, services, and offerings, giving users a broader understanding.

How long does it take to build a custom website? ▾

The timeline depends on the project's complexity. Simple designs may take a few weeks, while more complex, multi-page designs or integrations can take several weeks to months. We’ll work closely with you to define the timeline upfront.

Will my website be mobile-friendly? ▾

Absolutely. All websites we develop are responsive and optimized to provide an excellent user experience across all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Do you offer support after the website is launched? ▾

Yes, we provide ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website stays up-to-date, secure, and fully functional.

Can I update the content on my website after it’s launched? ▾

No, though you can speak to one of our advisors and we are happy to assit you in making the changes needed to your website

How much does a custom website cost? ▾

The cost varies depending on the size and complexity of the project. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed pricing information tailored to your needs.

Do you provide e-commerce solutions? ▾

Yes, we specialize in building secure, user-friendly e-commerce platforms that make it easy for you to sell your products or services online.

Will you optimize my website for search engines (SEO)? ▾

Yes, we include basic SEO optimization in all our website packages to ensure your site ranks better in search engine results.

Do you handle domain registration and hosting? ▾

At BlankPortalStudios we are able to obtain and register a domain name of your choice, we can then proceed with the setup to get you online with an official domain name.
