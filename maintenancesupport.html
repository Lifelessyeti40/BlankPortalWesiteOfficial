Maintenance & Support FAQs

1. What does website maintenance include? +

Website maintenance includes updates, backups, security monitoring, and ensuring the site runs smoothly without errors.

2. How often should my website be updated? +

Regular updates are essential. Core updates, plugins, and content should be reviewed at least once a month.

3. Do you provide 24/7 support? +

We offer support during business hours and emergency services for critical issues outside these times.

4. What happens if my website goes down? +

Our monitoring system will alert us immediately, and we’ll work to restore your site as quickly as possible.

5. Can I request changes to my site content? +

Yes, content updates are included in our support plans. Simply let us know the details, and we’ll make the changes for you.

6. Is website maintenance necessary if I have hosting? +

Yes, hosting ensures uptime and performance, but maintenance keeps your site secure, updated, and functional.
