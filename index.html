Frequently Asked Questions

What types of websites do you build?

We specialize in a variety of websites, including business sites, e-commerce platforms, portfolios, and blogs. Whether you need a simple landing page or a complex online store, we’ve got you covered.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for website development can vary depending on the project's complexity. Typically, a standard website takes between 4 to 8 weeks, but we’ll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after the website is launched?

Yes! We offer various support packages to ensure your website runs smoothly after launch. This includes maintenance, updates, and troubleshooting.

What if I already have a website but need a redesign?

No problem! We can assess your current website and create a redesign that enhances its functionality and appearance while retaining important elements that work well for your audience.

How much does it cost to build a website?

The cost of building a website varies based on features, design, and complexity. We provide custom quotes based on your specific needs after our initial consultation.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes! All our websites are designed with responsive design principles, ensuring they look great and function well on all devices, including smartphones and tablets.
